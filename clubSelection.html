Happy Club

Happy Club of SVECW is dedicated to the promotion of happiness and well-being among the student community. This club focuses on providing activities and events that uplift and bring joy to students, creating a positive environment on campus.

Dance Club

The idea behind establishing the club is to promote dance. It was formed to bring out the dancing talents and give a platform to those who have a passion and love for dance. It not only focuses on entertaining people but also works on bringing out motivational messages that will be useful for the students.

Photography Club

The Photography Club provides the best platform to ponder on the social issues combined with diverse talents of students. In this way, students get a chance to showcase their talents as well. It makes most of the students participate voluntarily in various events conducted in SVECW.
